Vegan Ramen

Description

This recipe is umami-packed

Ingredients

  • 1 block of firm tofu
  • 16 onces of whole white mushrooms
  • 1 large daikon radish
  • Vietnamese pho spices
  • Korean fermented soysauce yondu
  • onions, garlic, ginger

Steps

  1. Cut ginger, onion, and garlic head in half and char the surface on a pan, then add the spices until you can smell the aroma
  2. Add the daikon and mushroom and 3 cups of water to cover the pot. Simmer for 1 hour, should release the umami
  3. season the broth to taste
  4. In a bowl, add noodles, tofu, garnish and broth to serve
